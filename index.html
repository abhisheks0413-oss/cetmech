About Department of Mechanical Engineering

Widely recognized as a benchmark of engineering excellence in the state, the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the College of Engineering Trivandrum commands a distinguished history of academic success and pioneering breakthrough research. The department bridges foundational mechanical theory with high-end computational research platforms, advanced automotive labs, and experimental aerodynamics instrumentation facilities. Backed by an accomplished cohort of expert faculty members, our curriculum consistently produces industry-ready leaders who excel globally across technological fields.
